Why Warehousing?

In the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, as capital markets violently fluctuate and offices remain shut, the logistics sector has proven resilient and even growing. We believe that warehousing is one of the most lucrative, safe, and stable asset classes available to investors.

Points to Ponder

We list down some strong evidence which strengthens our believe in why Warehouses will be a huge asset class going forward

  • Global Supply chains are shifting away from China towards India
  • COVID-19 has hastened the adoption of eCommerce
  • Every $1bn in eCommerce sales, requires 1-1.5Mn sqft of warehousing
  • 3 million sqft of new warehousing was signed in the last few weeks including deals by Amazon and Flipkart
  • 3000+ acres are under various stages of closing
  • Over $600 million in institutional investments are underway
